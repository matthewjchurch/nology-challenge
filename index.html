Drum machine

One of the first projects I built in preparation of the course.

I used a constructor method to add classes to each beat, and built functions to visually represent the flow of each beat relative to the chosen tempo.

Clicking on a beat adds an active class, which means the relevant sound will play when reached in the flow.
